How to Play

Simple to Learn, Tricky to Master

Every hop counts. Plan your route, dodge your enemies, and reach the exit before your moves run out.

Hop Around

Move your frog in 8 directions across lily pads. Each level gives you a limited number of hops, so plan carefully!

Hit Your Goals

Visit numbered goal pads in order — 1, 2, 3 — before heading to the exit. Sequence matters!

Outsmart Enemies

Snakes pursue you, bees patrol the skies, and logs drift through the water. Every move they react!

Reach the Exit

After collecting all goals, hop to the final pad to complete the level. Sweet victory!

A World of Interactive Elements

Every level is a unique puzzle built from a toolbox of interactive tiles. One-use pads sink behind you, switches toggle bridges, and trampolines launch you across the board. Each element adds a new layer of strategy.
